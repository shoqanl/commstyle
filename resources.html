Practical Tips

For Direct Communicators Working with Indirect Communicators:

  • Pay attention to non-verbal cues and what is left unsaid
  • Build relationships before jumping into tasks
  • Be patient with indirect responses to questions
  • Avoid being too confrontational in conflicts

For Indirect Communicators Working with Direct Communicators:

  • Be more explicit and straightforward in your messages
  • Don't take direct communication as rudeness
  • Address conflicts openly rather than avoiding them
  • Focus on tasks and efficiency when needed
