Two Main Communication Styles

Direct / Low-Context

Task-oriented communication where messages are explicit and clear

  • Straightforward and explicit
  • Task-focused approach
  • Values punctuality
  • Direct conflict resolution

Common in: USA, Germany, UK, Netherlands

Indirect / High-Context

Relationship-oriented communication that relies on context and non-verbal cues

  • Context-dependent messages
  • Relationship-focused approach
  • Flexible time perception
  • Indirect conflict avoidance

Common in: Japan, Kazakhstan, Arab countries, China
