Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the assessment take?

The assessment typically takes 5-7 minutes to complete. There are 16 questions, each rated on a 5-point scale.

Is my data stored?

No, all responses are processed locally in your browser. We do not collect or store any personal information or assessment results.

Can I retake the assessment?

Yes! You can retake the assessment as many times as you'd like. Your communication style may evolve over time as you gain more cross-cultural experience.

Is one style better than the other?

No communication style is inherently better. Both direct and indirect styles have their strengths. The key is understanding your natural preference and learning to adapt when necessary.
